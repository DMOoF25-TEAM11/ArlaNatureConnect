# Casual Use Case – UC002B.5: Delete Farm

**Use case:** UC002B.5 – Delete Farm  
**Scope:** The prototype of the Arla Nature Connect system  
**Level:** User goal  
**Primary Actors:** Arla Employee (Admin / Sustainability Staff)

---

### **Stakeholders and Interests**

- **Arla employees** want to remove farms that are no longer relevant.

---

### **Preconditions**

- The Arla employee is authenticated (UC01).  
- The farm exists in the system.  
- The farm has no active Nature Check Cases.

---

### **Postconditions**

- The farm is removed from the system.  
- The farm no longer appears in the farm list.

---

### **Main Success Scenario**

1. The employee selects a farm to delete.  
2. The employee clicks delete and confirms.  
3. The system validates no active cases exist.  
4. The system deletes the farm.  
5. The system updates the farm list.

---

### **Extensions**

- **Farm has active cases:** System prevents deletion and shows error.

---

### **Special Requirements**

- Farms with active cases cannot be deleted.
- All database operations use stored procedures.
