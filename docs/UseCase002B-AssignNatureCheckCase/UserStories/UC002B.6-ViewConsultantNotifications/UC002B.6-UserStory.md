# User Story â€“ UC002B.6: View Consultant Notifications

**As a** consultant,  
**I want** to view all Nature Check Case assignments that have been assigned to me,  
**so that** I can see what work has been assigned and plan my visits accordingly.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I am an authenticated consultant  
   **When** I open the notifications view  
   **Then** I see all Nature Check Cases assigned to me

2. **Given** I am viewing my notifications  
   **When** the list is displayed  
   **Then** cases are sorted by assignment date (newest first)

3. **Given** I am viewing my notifications  
   **When** I see a notification  
   **Then** I can see the farm name, assignment date, priority, and notes

4. **Given** I have viewed a notification  
   **When** the notification is displayed  
   **Then** the notification is marked as read

---

## Notes

- This use case is for consultants to view their assigned cases
- Notifications are database-based (visible as badges in UI)
- Cases are sorted by assignment date with newest first
- Notifications are automatically marked as read when viewed
